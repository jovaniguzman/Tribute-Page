Marshall From Detroit

Marshall Mathers, better known as Eminem is one of the most lyrical rappers out there. He has accomplished so much since the beginning of his musical career. As a teenage dropout, Eminem found a way to express his passion for language, as well as to release his youthful anger, through the emerging musical genre of hip-hop.
